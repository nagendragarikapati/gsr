About Us

Your Home in
Hyderabad's Hub

GSR Executive Men's Hostel is strategically located in Cyber Hills Colony, Hitech City — the beating heart of Hyderabad's IT sector. Whether you're a working professional, a student, or someone new to the city, we offer more than just a bed: we offer a community.

Just minutes from Shilparamam, N Convention Road, and the HITEC City metro, our hostel puts you at the center of everything while giving you a comfortable, secure place to recharge.
